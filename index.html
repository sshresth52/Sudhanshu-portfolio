about

I am a forward-thinking Software Developer, holding a Bachelor of Technology from the Indian Institute of Technology, Indore. My expertise lies in full-stack development, with a versatile skill set that includes C++, Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Node.js, and React, complemented by a strong grasp of Data Structures, Algorithms, and Database Management Systems with MySQL. My educational journey began at the Rashtriya Military School, Bangalore, where I cultivated the resilience and discipline that fuel my results-driven approach to technology and innovation.

Eager to connect with like-minded professionals and tackle challenges that expand my skill set, I am committed to leveraging my expertise for impactful solutions and enduring success.
